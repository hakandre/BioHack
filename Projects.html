Who We Are

The team and our Goals

What We Do

Creating a web-based resource using genetic sequencing to compare and analyze specific diseases and disorders based on genetic mutations. Users can simply upload a DNA sequence onto the webpage and GeneHub identifies if there is a variant marker of a hereditary disorder such as Haemochromatosis, Huntington’s disease, or Alzheimer’s disease. Analyzing genetic markers is beneficial because it is a very accurate method of diagnosis.

Our Mission

We are a small organization working to find a convenient and accessible way to identify specific hereditary disorders. Based on specific sequences one can diagnose hereditary mutations faster and easier for those uninsured or unable to see a doctor. The benefit to society is to find diagnosis earlier in order to allocate more treatment options. This would provide a free resource for those of low income, the uninsured and people far from a doctor. We aim to make personal health awareness convenient, easy to use, free of cost, and available to everyone regardless of healthcare.
